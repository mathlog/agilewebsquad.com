Our Process

Contact

Contact us to let us know your need. If you provide enough information of what you have in mind, we will be able to provide a quote without further discussion.

Chat

We will call you back at a convenient time for you to chat about what you have in mind for your website.

Estimate

We will do a quick assessment of your needs and prepare a recommendation and an estimate of time and cost. We will also provide alternatives and the rationale for our recommendation.

Requirements

If you have specific requirements that the website must meet, we would get together briefly to discuss those. We like being proactive and showing an early prototype for ealy feedback this has proven to be the quickest route to success.

Demo

This is when we show you a prototype of your website, having taken into consideration your objectives, any specific requirements and feedback from the early prototype. At this stage we seek final feedback.

Launch

After the final touches, we will set up your website online and provide you with any information relevant to your website (as applicable).
